Last updated: 20 June 2026

About Crestly accounts

Crestly accounts are created and issued by your school, not by the app itself — there is no self sign-up. Your account and the records linked to it (attendance, leaves, salary, marks, fees, diary, timetable and contact details) belong to your school, which is the owner of that data. For this reason, deletion requests are handled together with your school.

How to request deletion of your account and data

To request that your Crestly account and its associated personal data be deleted, follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to it.crestly@gmail.com from the contact you use, with the subject "Account Deletion Request".
  2. In the email, include:
    • your full name,
    • the mobile number registered with the app,
    • the name of your school, and
    • which app you use — Crestly Staff or Crestly Parents.
  3. We will verify the request with you and your school, and confirm by email once the deletion is completed.

We respond to deletion requests within 30 days. If your school is legally required to retain certain records, we will tell you which data must be kept and for how long.
