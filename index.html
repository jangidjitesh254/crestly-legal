Privacy Policy

Crestly. Privacy Policy

Applies to the Crestly Staff (in.crestly.staff) and Crestly Parents (in.crestly.parents) mobile applications.

Last updated: 19 June 2026

1. Who we are

Crestly is a school management platform operated by Shadowbiz Startups ("Crestly", "we", "us", "our"). The Crestly apps are provided to schools so their staff and parents can access school information. Each school remains the owner ("data controller") of its own records; Crestly processes that data on the school's behalf to provide the service.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at it.crestly@gmail.com.

2. Scope

This policy explains what information the Crestly Staff and Crestly Parents apps collect, how it is used, and your choices. You access the apps using credentials issued through your school; the apps are not intended for use by children directly.

4. Camera & Location permissions

  • Camera (Staff app): used only to take the selfie required when you check in or out. The app never accesses the camera in the background.
  • Location (Staff app): used only at the moment of a check-in/out to measure your distance from the school and flag punches made outside the allowed radius. Location is not collected when you are not punching, and the app does not track your movements.

You can deny or revoke these permissions in your device settings; the related feature (selfie punch) will not work without them.

5. How we use your information

  • To authenticate you and provide the app's features.
  • To record and display attendance, leaves, salary, fees, marks, diary and timetable.
  • To verify staff attendance using the punch selfie and geofence check.
  • To process fee payments you initiate (through the payment gateway) and show receipts.
  • To keep the service secure and to comply with legal obligations.

6. Sharing & disclosure

  • Your school: staff and parent data is accessible to authorized administrators of the school you belong to, who own the records.
  • Payment processor: HDFC SmartGateway processes fee payments you make. Their handling of payment information is governed by their own privacy terms.
  • Service providers: trusted hosting/infrastructure providers that operate the Crestly service under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal: where required by law or to protect rights, safety and security.

7. Data security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access is restricted by role-based permissions and per-school isolation. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

8. Data retention

We retain information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the service, then in line with your school's record-keeping requirements and applicable law. You may request deletion as described below.

9. Children's privacy

The apps are used by adults — school staff and parents/guardians. Children do not create accounts or use the apps. Information about students is part of the school's records and is shown only to the authorized parent/guardian and school staff.

10. Your rights & choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. Because your school controls its records, please direct such requests to your school administrator, or contact us at it.crestly@gmail.com and we will assist the school in responding.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, through the app.
